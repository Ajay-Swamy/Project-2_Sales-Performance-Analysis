# Sales Performance Analysis

---

## Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
* Imported **Superstore sales dataset** containing details on orders, customers, products, categories, and geographical regions.

### 2. Data Cleaning
* Performed data quality and hygiene checks:
  * Missing value treatment
  * Duplicate checks
  * Date field conversion
  * Column standardization

### 3. Feature Engineering
* Extracted and generated time-based and operational attributes:
  * Month / Quarter / Year
  * Shipping Days (Order Date to Ship Date)
  * Custom Order Metrics

### 4. KPI Development
* Calculated critical performance metrics:
  * **Total Sales** & **Total Orders**
  * **Average Order Value (AOV)**
  * **Unique Customers** Count
  * **Average Shipping Days**

### 5. Sales Trend Analysis
* Analyzed macro and micro timeline patterns:
  * Monthly and Quarterly Sales trends
  * Seasonal sales fluctuations and spikes

### 6. Regional Performance Analysis
* Evaluated geographical sales distributions across:
  * Broad Regions
  * Specific States
  * Customer Locations / Hubs

### 7. Product Performance Analysis
* Categorized item performance to identify:
  * Top-performing and Bottom-performing products
  * High-level Category performance
  * Deep-dive Sub-Category performance

### 8. Customer Analysis
* Segmented and analyzed revenue contributions across business vectors:
  * Consumer Segment
  * Corporate Segment
  * Home Office Segment

### 9. Business Insights
* Uncovered key revenue drivers, isolated underperforming areas, and mapped out potential sales growth opportunities.

### 10. Recommendations
* Strategic formulations aimed at boosting:
  * Product Portfolio Performance
  * Regional Expansion efforts
  * Targeted Customer Acquisition
  * Overall Sales Growth

### 11. Dashboard Development
> **Final Deliverable:** Created an interactive **Power BI dashboard** showcasing core KPIs, historical Sales Trends, Regional mapping, and Product deep-dives.
